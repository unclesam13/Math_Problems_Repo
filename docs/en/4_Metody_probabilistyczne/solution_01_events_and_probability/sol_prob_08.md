# Solution 1 - Task 8 

## Given

Total pulses:

$$
4A, 2B, 1C
$$

Total:

$$
7
$$



## 1. First pulse is A

$$
P = \frac{4}{7}
$$



## 2. First pulse is A or C

Possible favorable pulses:

$$
4 + 1 = 5
$$

$$
P = \frac{5}{7}
$$



## 3. First two pulses are A then C

First:

$$
P(A_1) = \frac{4}{7}
$$

Second:

$$
P(C_2|A_1) = \frac{1}{6}
$$

Thus:

$$
P = \frac{4}{7}\cdot\frac{1}{6}
$$

$$
P = \frac{2}{21}
$$
