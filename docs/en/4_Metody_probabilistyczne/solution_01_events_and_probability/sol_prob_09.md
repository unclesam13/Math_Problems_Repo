# Solution 1 - Task 9

## Given

Given probabilities:

$$
P_1 = 0.97
$$

$$
P_2 = 0.90
$$

$$
P_3 = 0.86
$$

We select **one item from each plant**.

We want the probability that **all three are first quality**.

Assuming independence:

$$
P =
P_1 \cdot P_2 \cdot P_3
$$

Substitute:

$$
P =
0.97 \cdot 0.90 \cdot 0.86
$$

$$
P \approx 0.751
$$
